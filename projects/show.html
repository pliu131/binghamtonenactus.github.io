Coins for Change

Our Coins for Change project team works with an international nonprofit of the same name. The nonprofit's goal is to raise money for the Maasai tribes of Kenya, a semi-nomadic people at odds with the Kenyan government. Our team aims to increase the reach of their message and improve the sustainability of their actions while spreading the entrepreneurial spirit to the Maasai people.
